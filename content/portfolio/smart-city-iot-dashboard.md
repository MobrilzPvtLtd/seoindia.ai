## Project Overview

CityTech Solutions needed a centralized platform to monitor and manage smart city infrastructure including traffic lights, waste management, energy consumption, and public safety systems.

## System Components

### Traffic Management
- Real-time traffic flow monitoring
- Adaptive signal timing
- Incident detection and response
- Public transit integration
- Parking availability tracking

### Energy Management
- Smart grid monitoring
- Consumption analytics
- Renewable energy integration
- Load balancing
- Predictive maintenance

### Environmental Monitoring
- Air quality sensors
- Noise level monitoring
- Weather stations
- Flood detection systems
- Waste management optimization

### Public Safety
- Security camera integration
- Emergency response coordination
- Crowd monitoring
- Street lighting control

## Technical Implementation

- **Frontend**: Angular with real-time updates
- **Backend**: Python FastAPI for APIs
- **IoT Protocol**: MQTT for device communication
- **Time-Series DB**: InfluxDB for sensor data
- **Visualization**: Grafana dashboards
- **ML Models**: TensorFlow for predictions

## Impact

- **25% reduction** in traffic congestion
- **30% energy savings** in public lighting
- **40% faster** emergency response times
- **50% improvement** in waste collection efficiency
- **15% reduction** in carbon emissions

## Monitoring Capabilities

- **50,000+ IoT devices** connected
- **Real-time processing** of sensor data
- **Predictive maintenance** alerts
- **Historical analytics** and trends
- **Custom alerts** and notifications
